College Rules and Regulations

Welcome to our institution. Please adhere to the following rules and regulations to ensure a safe and productive environment for everyone.

1. Attendance

  • Students must attend at least 75% of their classes to be eligible for exams.
  • Attendance will be taken at the beginning of each class.
  • Medical leave must be supported by a valid medical certificate.

2. Academic Integrity

  • Cheating, plagiarism, and other forms of academic dishonesty are strictly prohibited.
  • Students caught cheating will face disciplinary action, which may include suspension or expulsion.
  • All assignments and projects must be completed by the student themselves.

3. Code of Conduct

  • Students are expected to behave respectfully towards faculty, staff, and fellow students.
  • Bullying, harassment, and discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
  • Appropriate dress code must be maintained at all times.

4. Campus Facilities

  • Students must take care of campus property and facilities.
  • Vandalism or misuse of campus resources will result in penalties.
  • Library books must be returned on time; late returns will incur fines.

5. Health and Safety

  • Smoking and the use of alcohol or illegal substances are strictly prohibited on campus.
  • Students must follow all safety protocols in laboratories and during fieldwork.
  • Emergency exits and fire extinguishers must be used only in emergencies.

For more details, please refer to the student handbook or contact the administration office.
